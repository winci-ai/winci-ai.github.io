About Me

My name is Xi Weng, and you can call me Winci. I am currently a first-year PhD student at the School of Computing, NUS, under the supervision of Prof. Jin-Song Dong. Prior to this, I earned both the Bachelor's and Master's degree from the School of Artificial Intelligence at Beihang University, supervised by Prof. Lei Huang. My primary research interests lie in self-supervised learning and multimodal pre-training.
